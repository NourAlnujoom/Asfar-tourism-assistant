Jordan Audio Guide

Listen to detailed audio guides about Jordan's most important historical and cultural sites. Perfect for learning while you travel or preparing for your visit.

Petra - The Rose City

Discover the ancient Nabatean city carved into rose-red sandstone cliffs. Learn about the Treasury, Monastery, and the fascinating history of this UNESCO World Heritage site.

Duration: 8:45

Wadi Rum - Valley of the Moon

Explore the vast desert landscapes that have inspired travelers for centuries. Learn about Bedouin culture, stargazing, and desert adventures in this stunning natural wonder.

Duration: 6:32

Dead Sea - The Lowest Point on Earth

Experience the therapeutic waters and mineral-rich mud of the Dead Sea. Learn about its unique properties, health benefits, and the best ways to enjoy this natural wonder.

Duration: 7:18

Jerash - The Pompeii of the East

Walk through one of the best-preserved Roman cities outside of Italy. Discover the impressive colonnaded streets, ancient theaters, and temples that tell the story of Roman Jordan.

Duration: 9:12

Amman Citadel - Heart of the Capital

Explore the ancient citadel that overlooks modern Amman. Learn about the city's rich history from the Bronze Age through the Islamic period, and enjoy panoramic views of the capital.

Duration: 5:45

Mount Nebo - Biblical Significance

Visit the mountain where Moses is said to have seen the Promised Land. Learn about its biblical importance and enjoy breathtaking views of the Dead Sea and Jordan Valley.

Duration: 4:28

Madaba - City of Mosaics

Discover the famous Byzantine mosaics, including the 6th-century map of the Holy Land. Learn about the city's Christian heritage and its importance in early cartography.

Duration: 6:15

Karak Castle - Crusader Fortress

Explore the impressive Crusader castle that once controlled the vital trade routes. Learn about the castle's strategic importance and the battles that shaped its history.

Duration: 7:52
