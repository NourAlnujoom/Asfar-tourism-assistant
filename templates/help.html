Getting Started

Welcome to Asfar! Here's how to get the most out of your Jordan tourism assistant:

  • Start with the Chatbot: Click on "Chatbot" in the navigation to begin planning your trip
  • Ask Specific Questions: The more specific your questions, the better our recommendations
  • Use Audio Guides: Listen to detailed information about Jordan's top sites
  • Explore All Features: Try different sections to discover everything Asfar offers

Using the Chatbot

Our chatbot is designed to help you plan the perfect Jordan trip. Here are some tips:

  • Trip Planning: Ask "Plan a 5-day trip to Jordan" or "What should I see in Petra?"
  • Weather & Crowds: Try "What's the weather like in Jordan?" or "When is Petra least crowded?"
  • Hidden Gems: Ask "Show me hidden gems" or "What do locals recommend?"
  • Practical Info: Ask about transportation, accommodation, or cultural etiquette

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Asfar free to use?

A: Yes! Asfar is completely free to use. We're here to help make your Jordan trip planning easier and more enjoyable.

Q: Do I need to create an account?

A: No account required! Asfar is designed to be accessible to everyone. Simply visit the website and start using our features.

Q: How accurate is the information?

A: We strive to provide the most current and accurate information about Jordan. However, we recommend double-checking specific details like opening hours and prices before your visit.

Q: Can I use Asfar offline?

A: The chatbot requires an internet connection, but you can download audio guides for offline listening during your travels.

Q: What languages are supported?

A: Currently, Asfar is available in English. We're working on adding Arabic and other languages in the future.

Technical Support

If you're experiencing technical issues:

  • Browser Compatibility: Asfar works best with modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Audio Issues: Make sure your device's audio is enabled and not muted
  • Slow Loading: Check your internet connection and try refreshing the page
  • Mobile Usage: Asfar is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices
