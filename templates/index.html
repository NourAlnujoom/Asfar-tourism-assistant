Why Choose Jordan?

Jordan is a land of ancient wonders and modern hospitality. From the iconic rock-carved city of Petra to the therapeutic waters of the Dead Sea, from the vast deserts of Wadi Rum to the bustling streets of Amman, Jordan offers experiences that will stay with you forever.

Our country is known for its warm hospitality, rich history, and diverse landscapes. Whether you're interested in archaeology, adventure, relaxation, or cultural immersion, Jordan has something special for every traveler.

Explore Jordan

Petra

The ancient Nabatean city carved into rose-red sandstone cliffs. A UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.

Wadi Rum

Experience the vast desert landscapes that have inspired travelers for centuries. Perfect for stargazing and desert adventures.

Dead Sea

The lowest point on Earth, famous for its therapeutic mud and the unique experience of floating in its mineral-rich waters.

Jerash

One of the best-preserved Roman cities outside of Italy, featuring impressive colonnaded streets and ancient theaters.

Amman

Jordan's vibrant capital city, where ancient history meets modern culture. Explore the Citadel, Roman Theater, and bustling souks.
